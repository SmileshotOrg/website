About Us

SmileShot is the future of dental photography workflow—meticulously crafted to bridge the gap between clinical precision and effortless organisation.
‍
Designed for modern dental practices, it empowers clinicians to capture, sort, and retrieve patient images with AI-enhanced intelligence. Say goodbye to cluttered folders and lost photos.
‍
With SmileShot, every photo finds its place—securely stored, beautifully indexed, and instantly accessible. Built for speed, clarity, and confidence in every patient interaction.
